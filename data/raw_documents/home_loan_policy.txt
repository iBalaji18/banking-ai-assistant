# Home Loan Policy Document
Effective Year: 2026

1. Interest Rates
- Floating Interest Rate: Starting at 8.35% p.a. for borrowers with a credit score above 750. For credit scores between 650-749, the rate is 8.85% p.a.
- Fixed Interest Rate: 9.25% p.a. for a lock-in period of 3 years.

2. Loan Amount and Tenure
- Maximum Loan Amount: Up to 90% of the property value for loans under 30 Lakhs, and 80% for loans above 30 Lakhs.
- Maximum Tenure: 30 years or until the age of 65 at loan maturity, whichever is earlier.

3. Processing Fees
- Standard Processing Fee: 0.5% of the loan amount or INR 10,000, whichever is higher, plus applicable GST.

4. Penalties and Foreclosure
- Late Payment Penalty: 2% per month on the overdue EMI amount.
- Foreclosure Charges: Nil for floating rate loans. For fixed rate loans, it is 2% of the principal outstanding if closed before the lock-in period.
- Part Prepayment: Minimum part prepayment amount should be equal to 2 EMIs. No charges on part prepayment for floating rate loans.
