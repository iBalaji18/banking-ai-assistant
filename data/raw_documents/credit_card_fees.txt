# Credit Card Schedule of Charges
Effective Year: 2026
Card Type: Premium Rewards Card

1. Joining and Annual Fees
- Joining Fee: INR 1,499 + GST.
- Annual Fee: INR 1,499 + GST from the second year onwards. Annual fee is waived if total spends in the previous year exceed INR 2,50,000.

2. Finance Charges
- Interest on Revolving Credit: 3.49% per month (41.88% per annum) on the outstanding amount.
- Minimum Amount Due (MAD): 5% of the total outstanding amount or INR 200, whichever is higher.

3. Penalties
- Late Payment Charges: 
  - Outstanding < 500: Nil
  - Outstanding 501-5000: INR 400
  - Outstanding 5001-10000: INR 750
  - Outstanding > 10000: INR 1000
- Cash Advance Fee: 2.5% of the withdrawn amount, subject to a minimum of INR 500.

4. Forex Markup
- International Transaction Markup: 2% on the transaction value.
